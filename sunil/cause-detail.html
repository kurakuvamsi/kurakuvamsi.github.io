Donate Now

Through kids’ donations, we encourage the next generation to practice generosity and compassion. By giving to national funds, children learn the value of sharing, supporting those in need, and being a blessing to the wider community.

This act of giving not only supports important causes at the national level but also helps children understand that their small contributions can create a big impact. “By involving kids in donation efforts, the church nurtures a spirit of kindness, unity, and responsibility, shaping them into future leaders with generous hearts.”

When children contribute, they take part in the mission of the church and learn that they too can be instruments of hope and change. Such initiatives strengthen the bond between faith, community, and nation—showing that even the youngest hearts can make a meaningful difference.
