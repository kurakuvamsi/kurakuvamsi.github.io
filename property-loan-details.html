Property Loan Details

Property Loan

At Bavya Sree Finance, we understand that purchasing or investing in property is one of the most important financial decisions you will ever make. Our property loan solutions are designed to help you secure the home, office space, or investment property you’ve always wanted, with flexible terms and competitive interest rates to fit your financial goals.

We offer loans for a variety of needs, including buying a new property, constructing a building, or renovating an existing one. Our process is transparent, with no hidden charges, and our documentation requirements are kept simple to make the journey as smooth as possible. Whether you’re a first-time buyer or an experienced investor, our team will guide you every step of the way.

With fast approvals, customized repayment options, and attractive interest rates, we make property ownership achievable and stress-free. We also provide mortgage loan options, enabling you to unlock the value of your property for other personal or business needs.

At Bavya Sree Finance, we believe in turning your real estate dreams into reality. Let us help you own the property you deserve while enjoying a seamless, trustworthy, and customer-focused loan experience.

Loan Requirements

To ensure a smooth and quick loan approval process, please prepare and submit the following documents:

  • Basic Identification & Address Proof – A valid government-issued photo ID (such as Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Passport, or Driving License) and proof of address.
  • Income Proof – Latest salary slips, bank statements, or income tax returns, depending on your employment type.
  • Property Details (if applicable) – Complete property information with photographs and soft copies of related documents for property or mortgage loans.
  • Insurance Details (if applicable) – Relevant insurance documents for loans with mortgage or vehicle coverage.
  • Other Supporting Documents – Any additional paperwork specified during the application process, including photocopies of all submitted documents.

    • Note: All documents must be valid, accurate, and submitted in both original (for verification) and photocopy

We offer loans at very reasonable interest rates with a simple documentation process, making it easy for you to get the financial support you need.
